# 🌱 Carbon Clarity : Carbon Footprint Transparency in E-Commerce A CASE STUDY

A product case study on how e-commerce platforms can turn mandatory carbon-disclosure regulation (EU CSRD, SEC climate rules) into a competitive advantage by 2035 — using progressive disclosure and layered UX design to give shoppers carbon transparency without hurting conversion.

Submitted to **HelloPM's Summer of Product 2025 Case Study Challenge** as **Team Gatherer**.

---

## TL;DR

By 2035, e-commerce platforms will be legally required to disclose the carbon footprint of every product. Done badly, this creates cognitive overload and tanks conversion. This case study reframes that compliance burden as a UX and business opportunity: a **layered disclosure system** (quick visual signals for everyone, deep verified data for those who want it), backed by user research, a phased MVP roadmap, and a risk-mitigation plan.
